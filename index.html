Focusing on the gourmet breakfast and lunches as well as the youth of India, Delight Café aims to bring to you a life changing experience. It is a quirky, chic, modern café, where guests enjoy a sensory experience through the finest modern cuisines, with a high energy ambiance. Delight Café presents to you a place where culinary art meets the alchemy of modern presentations and cooking techniques to absorb the guest into the ultimate gastronomic illusion.
